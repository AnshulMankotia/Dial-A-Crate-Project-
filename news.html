15 Easy Cocktails & Drinks

3 Jan, 2023

Cocktails and drinks are a great way to unwind and enjoy a delicious beverage. Whether you're hosting a party, relaxing with friends, or simply want to try something new, here are five easy cocktails and drinks that are sure to impress.

1. Mojito

The mojito is a classic cocktail that's perfect for any occasion. It's refreshing, delicious, and easy to make. To create a mojito, you'll need:

  • 2 oz. white rum
  • 1 oz. lime juice
  • 1 oz. simple syrup
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • Club
  • Ice

To make the cocktail, muddle the mint leaves with the lime juice and simple syrup in a glass. Add the rum and stir well. Fill the glass with ice and top with club soda. Garnish with a sprig of mint and a lime wedge.

2. Margarita

The margarita is another classic cocktail that's easy to make and always a crowd-pleaser. To make a margarita, you'll need:

  • 2 oz. tequila
  • 1 oz. lime juice
  • 1 oz. triple sec or Cointreau
  • Salt
  • Ice

To make the cocktail, rim a glass with salt. Fill the glass with ice. In a shaker, combine the tequila, lime juice, and triple sec. Shake well and strain into the glass. Garnish with a lime wedge.

3. Moscow Mule

The Moscow mule is a refreshing cocktail that's perfect for a hot summer day. To make a Moscow mule, you'll need:

  • 2 oz. vodka
  • 1 oz. lime juice
  • Ginger beer
  • Ice

To make the cocktail, fill a copper mug with ice. Add the vodka and lime juice. Top with ginger beer and stir. Garnish with a lime wedge.

4. Sangria

Sangria is a delicious wine-based cocktail that's perfect for a party. To make a pitcher of sangria, you'll need:

  • 1 bottle of red wine
  • 1/2 cup brandy
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 sliced orange
  • 1 sliced lemon
  • 1 sliced lime
  • Club soda
  • Ice

To make the cocktail, combine the wine, brandy, orange juice, sugar, and sliced fruit in a pitcher. Stir well and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Add ice and top with club soda before serving.

5. Lemonade

Lemonade is a classic non-alcoholic drink that's perfect for a hot summer day. To make lemonade, you'll need:

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice

To make the drink, combine the lemon juice, sugar, and water in a pitcher. Stir well and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Add ice before serving.

Conclusion

In conclusion, these five cocktails and drinks are easy to make and perfect for any occasion. Whether you're in the mood for a classic cocktail or a refreshing non-alcoholic drink, these recipes are sure to impress. So go ahead, give them a try and enjoy!
